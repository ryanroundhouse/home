Privacy Policy for Turf Wars

Effective date: April 7, 2026

Turf Wars collects certain information to operate the game and support account recovery. This may include an app installation identifier, approximate and precise location-related data, nearby Wi-Fi and cellular network identifiers, optional recovery email addresses, and push notification tokens.

Sensitive gameplay signal data, including location coordinates and nearby network identifiers, is encrypted before it leaves your device and is stored in encrypted form on the server. Certain other data, such as chat message contents, is also encrypted in transit and at rest.

We may share data with third-party service providers that help us operate Turf Wars. These providers may include Google Firebase for push notifications, Google Maps when location links are opened or generated, and others.

You may request deletion of your account information by contacting us at ryangraham.ca/contact.
